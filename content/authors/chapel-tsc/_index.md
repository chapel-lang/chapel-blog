The Chapel Technical Steering Committee (TSC) is the group that
formally governs the Chapel language project.  Though most project
decisions are made using an informal consensus-based approach, those
related to governance, or requiring a more formal vote, are decided by
the TSC.  For further information about the committee, including a
list of its current members, please refer to the project's
[GOVERNANCE.md](https://github.com/chapel-lang/governance/blob/main/GOVERNANCE.md)
file.
